Research

I am currently at Université Paris Cité (MAP5), working with Julie Delon and Eloi Tanguy on optimal transport between Gaussian mixtures.

Last year, I developed efficient algorithms for tropical support vector machines with Xavier Allamigeon, Stéphane Gaubert, and Théo Molfessis.

Before that, at the Centro de Modelamiento Matemático (CMM), I investigated online algorithms for combinatorial auctions with José Correa and Andrés Cristi. In 2023, at Exotec, I designed control systems that doubled robot speeds.

I contribute to the Python Optimal Transport library.
